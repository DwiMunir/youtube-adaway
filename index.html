Privacy and Policy

Introduction

Welcome to YouTube AdAway! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy and Policy document outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

YouTube AdAway collects minimal data to function effectively:

  • Installation Date: We store the date when you first install the extension to manage the 30-day trial period.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Manage Trial Period: Track the installation date to determine when the 30-day free trial ends.

Data Storage and Security

We store your data locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We do not transmit your data to external servers. The data is protected by Chrome's security features, ensuring that only the extension can access the stored information.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with any third parties. Your data is solely used by the YouTube AdAway extension to provide and improve our services.

User Control and Data Retention

You have control over your data:

  • Clear Data: You can clear the extension's data at any time through Chrome's settings.
  • Uninstall Extension: Uninstalling the extension will remove all stored data from your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this page. Continued use of the extension after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.
